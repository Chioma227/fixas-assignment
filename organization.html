Our Units

Engineering/Development Team

Software Engineers/Developers

Our developers are responsible for writing code, designing software systems, and ensuring that the product functions correctly.

Quality Assurance/Testers

These professionals test software to identify and fix bugs and ensure the product meets quality standards.

DevOps Engineers

They focus on the deployment, automation, and management of software development processes.

Database Administrators (DBAs)

They manage and maintain the company's databases, ensuring data security and integrity.

Product Management

Product Managers

They define the product roadmap, prioritize features, and act as a liaison between different teams.

Product Designers/UX/UI Designers

They create user-friendly interfaces and design product features based on user research and feedback.

Sales and Marketing

Sales Representatives/Sales Engineers

They engage with potential clients, understand their needs, and sell the company's products or services.

Marketing Managers

They develop marketing strategies, run campaigns, and promote the company's products to attract customers.

Content Writers/Copywriters

They create compelling content for marketing materials, websites, and social media.

Customer Support

Customer Support Representatives

Our customer service representatives assist customers with product-related inquiries, troubleshoot issues, and provide solutions.

Operations

Operations Managers

They oversee the day-to-day operations of the company, including budgeting, resource allocation, and project management.

HR (Human Resources)

They handle recruitment, employee relations, training, and benefits administration.

Finance

They manage the company's financial activities, including budgeting, accounting, and financial reporting.

Research and Development (R&D)

Researchers/Scientists

They explore new technologies, conduct experiments, and innovate to develop new products or improve existing ones.
